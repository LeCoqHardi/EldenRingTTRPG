The Project :

Welcome to the official website of *Elden Ring: The Tabletop Role-Playing Game*—a dark and immersive journey into the world of the Lands Between. Explore the depth and lore of Elden Ring in a new and captivating way, where you become the Tarnished, embarking on perilous quests, battling formidable foes, and seeking to restore order to a fractured world. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or new to the realm of TTRPGs, our site offers everything you need: from game rules and character creation guides to detailed maps and exclusive content, bringing the hauntingly beautiful universe of Elden Ring to life on your tabletop.
